# Royal Canin HQ metric retail packshots

Current official Royal Canin UK metric retail catalog snapshot. Dog and cat products are included; veterinary products are excluded.

- `manifest.csv` maps every listed pack size/EAN to its official HQ packshot and source page.
- `images/dog` and `images/cat` contain downloaded originals.
- `image_scope` identifies whether the image is weight-specific or a shared official master.
- Images and trademarks remain the property of their respective rights holders; verify commercial reuse permission.
