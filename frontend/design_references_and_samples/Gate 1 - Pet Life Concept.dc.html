OutPlanets as category navigation · two homepage doors · delivery milestones as the core game · رهرو/ستاره‌شناس roles · generic XP & daily missions · a separate world to enter

KeptPersian-first RTL · warm neutral commerce surfaces · deep indigo reserved for immersive moments · amber/brass progress accents · evidence-led trust language · explicit prices & transaction facts

IA PRINCIPLES
One home, not two doors. The pet-life home is the default landing surface; shop and orders are ordinary siblings, not a rival world.
Metaphor is a skin, not a map. Removing the habitat artwork leaves a fully usable utility page.
Night surfaces are earned. Deep indigo appears only in seasonal expeditions and سفر سفارش — a treat, not wallpaper.
Facts stay literal. Prices, expiry, sourcing, payment: always plain Persian, never inside the story layer.

SHARED RULES
Identical architecture and data across all three; only the habitat strip's skin differs.
Progress comes from real journeys, records and learning — never generic XP or owner levels.
Rewards: habitat objects, illustrations, memory cards, story chapters, pet badges, planning capabilities. Wallet credit is not an automatic reward.
The strip never implies health status; it reflects care activity only.
Recommendation: M1 باغ ایرانی as primary direction, M3 reserved for seasonal expeditions.
