These files are the final visual and interaction references for coding.

## Accepted high-fidelity page waves

- Wave 1: shell/Today, authentication, onboarding, and shop.
- Wave 2: cart, checkout, Today attention, inventory, and reorder.
- Wave 3: account, support, diary, Garden, and journey.
- Wave 4: OTP exceptions, address, shop list/compare/subscription, payment error, confirmation, and order history/timeline.
- Wave 5: wallet, notifications, privacy, and the full journey lifecycle.
- Wave 6: loading, empty, network, policy-error, and stale-data states.

## Implementation qualifications

- Quiet-hours presentation is accepted, but persistence is deferred until a read-back contract exists.
- Product imagery and any missing custom visual assets remain asset-supplied content, not a reason to invent emoji or production artwork.
- Exact care ratios and escalation copy are server-managed approved content. The frontend renders typed content and does not encode clinical rules.
- Compensation, reserve, refund/replacement/substitution, post-sourcing cancellation, and push UI remain hidden while their policies are disabled.
- The `.dc.html` files are visual references, not production source code. `support.js` is preview-only.
