# ADR-003 — Sourcing boundary and launch offer mode

**Status:** Approved  
**Date:** 2026-07-16

Free cancellation ends when the supplier order has been financially committed, timestamped,
and supported by evidence. A bare operator status change is insufficient.

Launch exposes full-payment offers only. The domain model may represent `Reserve now`, but the
capability remains disabled until its payment, expiry, approval, and customer-rights policy is
approved.
